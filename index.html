Understanding Dandruff: Causes and Solutions

Dandruff is a common scalp condition characterized by the shedding of dead skin cells, often resulting in visible white or yellow flakes in the hair. It is frequently accompanied by itching and irritation, making it both uncomfortable and, at times, embarrassing. Understanding the causes of dandruff is an essential step toward finding the right solutions to manage this condition.
